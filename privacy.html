Last updated: July 2026

When you use our contact form or cost estimator, we may collect:

We use the information solely to respond to your inquiries, prepare project proposals, and communicate with you about your request. We do not use it for automated advertising.

Your submissions are stored securely on encrypted cloud infrastructure (Supabase). Access is restricted to authorized team members only.

4. Sharing Your Information

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except when required by law.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
